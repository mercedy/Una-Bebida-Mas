This beer-brand is founded by three students. Three students that were looking for a way-out from their busy lives. They were looking for a sanctuary, something that gives them the feeling of being on a holiday in Mexico, without any responsibilities, worries or absurdities. Worries such as feeling lonely and a bit down because of being in lockdown during a pandemic. Absurdities such as harmful stereotypes and discrimination between genders and races that we are, as a brand, are poised to fight. We were absolutely sure that it was not just us who thinks the same as us sometimes, and so, ‘Una Bebida Mas’ was born. With just one sip of ‘Una Bebida Mas’ our customers will awake in the party spirit and excited to celebrate. Celebrate the feeling of being on a holiday, without responsibilities or care about the hustle and bustle of life. Before we know it, customers will scream ‘Una Bebida Mas’, screaming for another glass of our tequila flavoured beer together with a new community of other ‘Una-Bebida Mas’ drinkers’.
