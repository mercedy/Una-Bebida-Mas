Communication messages and objectives

With our social campaign we wanted to reach as many early adopters as possible. Our brand is user oriented, has benefits such as social acceptance, breaking free from day-to-day life and feeling like on a holiday. All of our communication strategy is based on our persona. The campaign is about engaging our consumers, show them that we stand behind their values, attract open-minded people. .

Objectives.

  • Reach objective

    Reach objectives – For Instagram we wanted to reach 2 thousand people. For TikTok we wanted to reach 4 thousand views in total.

  • Affect objective

    When our message reaches our audience mainly, we wanted them to feel social acceptance, safe feeling and a lit bit of excitement for our product.

  • Response objective

    We want our target audience to start follow what we are posting seeing our next moves. Main goal of course is to achieve more sales for our product. And we want to do that with delivering a clear message or brand stands for social acceptance and having a good time after a long week.

Target Audience

Our target audience is middle-class millennials. From Western Europe who leads an active lifestyle and enjoys beer Researches the brand they are going to buy from beforehand. Interested in brand beliefs (environmentally conscious, diversity) and are heavy social media users. Expects responsive and well thought out social media posts and website. Enjoys hearing feedback from other people before buying the product from a particular brand. Decision stage is heavily based on other people feedback .
