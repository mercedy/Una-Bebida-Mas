Communication messages and objectives 

With our social campaign we wanted to reach as many early adopters as possible. Our brand is user oriented, has benefits such as social acceptance, breaking free from day-to-day life and feeling like on a holiday. All our communication strategy is based on our persona. 

The campaign is about engaging our consumers, show them that we stand behind their values, attract open-minded people              

  • Reach objective

    100 followers on Instagram, 2000 views on Instagram reels 4000 views on TikTok in total

  • Affect objective

    As our main message is to be able to have good types without worrying about any stereotypes, we wanted our target audience to feel accepted and free.

  • Response objective

    Our main goal is to build a strong and loyal community, to find people who truly agree with our brand message. We want our target audience to believe in what we are fighting for and be able to do it by buying our product. As our end goal is to increase our sales. We deliver clear message – we stand for social acceptance and having a good time after a long week.  

Target Audience

Our target audience is middle-class millennials. From Western Europe who lead an active lifestyle and enjoys beer. Our target audience researches the brand they are going to buy from beforehand. Interested in brand beliefs. Expects responsive and well thought out social media posts and website. Enjoys hearing feedback from other people before buying the product from a particular brand. Decision stage is heavily based on other people feedback.  

  • What platforms/social media does your target group use?

    In 2020 most used social media platforms for Dutch millennials were WhatsApp, Facebook and YouTube with Instagram taking the 4th place. 

  •  How much do they use it? And How long?

    In the research conducted in X 54% of the respondents answered that they spend from 1 to 3 h per day on social media while only 12% qualified as light users (<1h) 

  • What is their activity on social media platforms?  

    Millennials are ranking the highest from all age groups with Seventy-two percent stating that social is an essential part of their lives and Seventy-five percent of Millennials say that social media enables them to interact with brands and companies.  This age group has high costumer service expectations while at the same time expressing the most love to the brand if they are happy with the service compared to other age groups. Regarding social media platforms, 52.2% follow self-selected brands on Instagram, 37.9% on Facebook and the remainder on e.g. YouTube or Snapchat.

  • What other brands do they like?

    When looking at beer ranking millennials rate Blue Moon as their first choice and Corona extra as second.

  • How should you position your message?

    Our brand as previously mentioned is user oriented therefore, we position or message as an experience our consumer needs to feel free while having a benefit of supporting social acceptance, a value UNA BEBIDA mas stands by. Our brand wants to communicate a possible escape from the struggles of real world. A sip of our beer awakens the party spirit in the user and excites to celebrate, celebrate both the absurdities and pleasures of life. Since absurdities do exist, we as a brand are poised to fight some of them, mainly unnecessary and harmful stereotypes and discrimination between genders and races. Our drinkers cheer to bring about a party spirit while inviting social change at the forefront.

Media Tactics

We chose online marketing for our brand because of the low costs in comparison of people reached with the campaign and as previously mentioned our target audience is heavy social media users. We used two social media platforms, Instagram and TikTok. In 2020 most used social media platforms amongst millennials Instagram took 4th place and TikTok only 9th . (See appendix nr.4) Most respondents did say that despite that Facebook ranks as the second-most popular social media platform, they use Facebook much less than they used to. While usage of Instagram has rapidly increased over the years. As for TikTok in 2020 it had just launched and already ranked higher than other social media platforms that had been there for years. As a new brand our main objective is to send out our message to as many people as possible therefore, we chose platforms where you can do that the best. TikTok videos will help us to reach large number of people without having followers. Same for Instagram reels. Content quality and brand interactivity both have a positive and significant impact on brand awareness, however the effect of content quality is stronger.(7) Therefore, it will be crucial to make our content original and good quality. Later on, we will post a giveaway on both platforms to engage followers in order to gain people attention and make our campaign more engaging for consumers. We will also use most used hashtags in both platforms to help reach more people. As for TikTok we will choose popular and at that time “trendy” sounds.

Learning points

 Throughout the campaign we struggled with keeping up with our content calendar. It took much more time to make qualitative posts then we first thought. We didn’t complete two out of three of our reach objectives – 100 followers on Instagram, 4000views on TikTok in total. We had 33 followers on Instagram and 782 views in total for our tree TikTok posts. Although we did reach the objective for 2000 views on Instagram reels, we got 3135 views. Because of time limitation we didn’t make enough posts to build engaging followers, we had 50 followers at first but then many of them unfollowed. We did make successful Instagram reel with reaching 3014accounts. But the second one fell flat with having no views at all. From that we learned that it is crucial to post the reel in the feed as a post as well. The use of hashtags worked quite well, for our first TikTok we got 489 views without using a well-known TikTok sound, only three hashtags, two of them being most used in TikTok. For our next two we had 146 views for each. We believe, that those two didn’t get as many views because they were posted in different time, earlier in the day. There wasn’t a lot that went as expected, there was a lot of milestones and mistakes to learn from. The first one being not enough posts. Now we would create more and pay more attention to the time when posting our content. One thing that went as expected was delivering high quality content, sending a clear message and being consistent with our design that closely reflected our brand identity.
